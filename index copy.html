Introduction

VotanicXR is a multi-platform extended reality (XR) creation platform featuring a suite of tools and libraries to help developers of all levels to build XR applications with unified user experience across multiple platforms.

Here, we provide a range of useful information to help developers gain a holistic understanding of VotanicXR and how its features can be utilized to aid XR creation in various use cases. The information is put together and structured to provide a gentle learning curve to developers of all familiarity level to XR creation, with additional documentation available for advanced developers who are intrigued to learn and use VotanicXR beyond its typical use cases.
